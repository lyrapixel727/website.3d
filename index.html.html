Our Story

Built on a wood-fired grill and a short, honest menu.

Wildwood Table opened in 2019 with a simple idea: cook a handful of dishes exceptionally well, source everything within a day's drive, and let the grill do most of the talking.

Our menu changes with the seasons and the farmers we work with — never longer than one page, always worth the trip.
